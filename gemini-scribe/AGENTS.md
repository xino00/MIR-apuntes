## Organization

The vault is organized using a structured numerical system that prioritizes clinical utility and knowledge retrieval. The hierarchy includes:

- **Knowledge Repositories**: Folders like `10_Patologias` and `20_Farmacos` store foundational medical data.
- **Clinical Practice**: `30_Urgencias`, `40_Casos clinicos`, and `50_Guardias` capture practical experience, shift logs, and specific medical 'pearls'.

- **Standardization**: A robust `99_Plantillas` folder ensures consistency across pathology notes, drug profiles, and clinical logs through the use of predefined templates.
